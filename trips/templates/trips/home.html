About the Project

Redefining the Way You Travel

Travel World is a modern web application designed to simplify the chaotic process of trip planning. Whether you are a solo backpacker or traveling with family, our platform provides a structured way to manage your time and money.

We believe that travel should be about the experience, not the stress of managing spreadsheets. Our smart engine auto-generates itineraries based on your budget and destination, ensuring you never miss a local landmark or hidden gem.

With real-time image integration via Unsplash API, you get a visual feel of your destination even before you arrive. This project integrates Python, Django, and Bootstrap to deliver a seamless user experience across all devices.

Our budget tracker keeps a close eye on your expenses, dividing your total budget into daily limits so you can enjoy your vacation without financial worries.

Security is our priority. With Django’s robust authentication system, your trip data is safe and private. You can customize, update, and track your travel status with just a few clicks, making it the most reliable tool for travelers.
